Learning Opportunities


The children's learning time is modeled after the Creative Curriculum Standards.

Infants and todlers:
  • Instruction is gained through play. I offer many differant activities and toys from which to choose.

Older toddlers and pre-school:
  • Instruciton is gained through play. Their play is their work.
  • Play materials that are more focused on interaction for specific development areas.
  • Through our interaction and play, I will direct them toward patterns, sorting, adding, subtracting, writing, reading, logical thinking, problem solving, physical, social and emotional development.

I will be using Teaching Strategies Gold to assess your child's progress and development in all areas. You will have two opportunities one in fall and one in spring to attend a Parent Teacher Conference. We will use this time to discuss your child's progress and development, and also discuss future goals.
