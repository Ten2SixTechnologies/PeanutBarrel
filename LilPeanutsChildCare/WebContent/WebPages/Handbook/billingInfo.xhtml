Payment Options
  • I will accept check, cash, money order or State/DHS pay. If paying via State/DHS, any unpaid balance is the parent's responsibility. This balance must be paid within one week of notice.
  • Payment is required on the first day of attendance each week for the entire week. Bi-weekly and monthly payments may be arranged.
  • A $5.00 late fee will be charged to your account daily if the account is not paid in full as scheduled.
  • NON-SUFFICIENT FUNDS - You Will be billed $40.00 for any check returned.
  • After two NSF charges, ONLY cash or money order will be accepted.

Attendance
  • You must submit the following week's schedule by Friday of the previous week.
  • First day absent of each week, and any schedule changes, require payment unless I am notified at least 3 days in advance. Any consecutive absences that same week will not be charged.
  • If you are late picking up your child, there will be a $5.00 charge after 15 minutes, and $5.00 for every part of 15 minutes thereafter.
  • You will need to inform me of any vacation time your child will be taking no less than one week in advance to avoid billing for that time.
